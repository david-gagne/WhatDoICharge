How To Use

1. Enter a number for a target revenue (That's the amount of money you want to make).

2. Select the payment processor you'll use from the dropdown menu.

3. Feast your eyes on the price you need to charge!

About WhatToCharge

I made this little calculator to help people figure out how much they need to charge their customers to cover the fees deducted by payment processors.
It's a static website built using Semantic UI for the page elements and views. I hope that it brings you many moments of mathematical elation. Figuring out the formula was certainly fun for me .
